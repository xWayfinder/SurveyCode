Continuous Delivery Assessment

Continuous Delivery is set of practices and principles aimed at building, testing and releasing software faster and more frequently, with increased quality and greater visibility. These practices and principles can help your organization by improving speed to market, reducing costs and overheads in development and test, and enabling your products to be released more frequently to potentially allow for generating more revenue.
